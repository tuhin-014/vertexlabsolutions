NerveCalm — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Overview

NerveCalm ("the App") is a nervous system wellness tool developed by Vertex Lab Solutions. Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device only:

  • Mood check-in entries and nervous system state logs
  • Breathing exercise session history and duration
  • Somatic exercise completion records
  • Heart rate measurements (camera-based, never transmitted)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Guided program progress

All personal wellness data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any external server.

Camera Access

The App may request camera access solely for the heart rate biofeedback feature. Camera data is processed locally in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App uses local notifications scheduled on your device. No push notification server is involved.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Analytics & Tracking

NerveCalm does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your behavior or share data with advertisers.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third party. The Therapist Export feature generates a PDF report locally that you choose to share at your discretion.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored on your device. Deleting the App removes all associated data. There is no server-side data to delete.

Children's Privacy

NerveCalm is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
