Privacy Policy — DopaFlow

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Developer: VertexLab Solutions LLC

Overview

DopaFlow ("the App") is an ADHD focus timer and productivity tool. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

DopaFlow stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • Focus sessions, tasks, and settings — stored locally on your device only.
  • No accounts or sign-in — the App does not require any account creation.
  • No analytics or tracking — we do not use any third-party analytics services.
  • No third-party AI — all task breakdown features use on-device algorithms only.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional subscriptions and a lifetime purchase processed by Apple. Purchase data is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications for focus session reminders. These are processed entirely on your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
